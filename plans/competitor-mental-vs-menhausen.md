## A Deeper Look

### Anonymity & Privacy

**Mental** requires account creation with email and full name. Sessions, journal entries, and AI coach chats are tied to that identity. There is no anonymous or guest mode.

**Menhausen** requires no account, no email, no name, and no phone verification. Stress cards and history are encrypted with AES-256 and live on your device, not a profile.

**Bottom line**: Choose Mental if you want named, persistent progress you can revisit. Choose Menhausen if privacy is non-negotiable.

### Platform & Access

**Mental** is a native iOS and Android app. You download it, sign up, and the full experience lives in the app. There is no web or Telegram version.

**Menhausen** is a Telegram bot. Tap a link, hit Start, and you're in. No app store, no storage, no updates — works on any device that runs Telegram.

**Bottom line**: Choose Mental if you prefer a dedicated app icon. Choose Menhausen if you want zero friction and zero install.

### Methodology

**Mental** blends AI coaching, audio content, gamified 'protocols' (cold shower, push-ups), and 1,000+ audiobook summaries. It's content-rich but methodology-light — there is no CBT or ACT framework you progress through.

**Menhausen** is built on six evidence-based techniques from CBT and ACT: cognitive reframing, somatic anchoring, values clarification, behavioral activation, acceptance, and defusion. Each card walks you through a specific protocol.

**Bottom line**: Choose Mental if you want a broad library of motivation and audio. Choose Menhausen if you want structured, clinically-validated techniques.

### Session Length

**Mental**'s AI coach runs 15-minute sessions. Protocols and audiobooks range from 5 to 30 minutes. Designed for longer, planned engagement.

**Menhausen** — every stress card is 3–7 minutes, designed for the moment stress hits — between meetings, on the train, after an argument.

**Bottom line**: Choose Mental if you have time to sit down. Choose Menhausen if stress hits you between meetings.

### Pricing

**Mental** — free trial (typically 7 days), then a subscription of ~$9.99/month or $59.99/year. No permanent free tier — credit card required after the trial ends.

**Menhausen** — free forever: 10+ basic stress cards and daily check-in. Premium is $4.99/month for advanced modules, audio guides, and unlimited history.

**Bottom line**: Choose Mental if you want a polished library and don't mind paying. Choose Menhausen if you want a permanent free option and roughly half the premium price.
