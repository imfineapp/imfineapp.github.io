Somatic Anchoring

A body-based technique that uses physical sensations to ground yourself in the present moment. It helps interrupt stress spirals by shifting focus from racing thoughts to bodily experience.

Benefits

  • ✓Quickly calm acute stress or anxiety
  • ✓Ground yourself in overwhelming situations
  • ✓Build awareness of mind-body connection
  • ✓Create personal anchors for calm states

How It Works

  1. 1Notice your current physical sensations
  2. 2Focus on points of contact (feet on floor, body in chair)
  3. 3Name what you see, hear, and feel in your body
  4. 4Press fingertips together and breathe deeply

Questions to Ask Yourself

"What sensations do you notice in your body right now?"

"Where do you feel stress manifesting physically?"

"What does your body need right now?"

"Can you name 5 things you can see, 4 you can hear?"
